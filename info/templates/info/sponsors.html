    Breachforce

    BreachForce is an open security community of security researchers, ethical hackers, and cybersecurity professionals dedicated to advancing offensive security, red teaming, and penetration testing. We organize monthly in-person meet-ups in Mumbai, where professionals, cybersecurity enthusiasts, and beginners discuss on latest threats, emerging security trends, methodologies, exploits, and defensive strategies. Our community emphasizes collaborative knowledge sharing, practical hands-on skill development, and real-world security exercises. BreachForce empowers individuals to elevate their cybersecurity capabilities, grow their professional networks, and excel in their security careers.

    HackTheBox

    Hack The Box (HTB) is a global cybersecurity community and training platform where enthusiasts and professionals sharpen their real-world skills in a virtual battleground. Through hands-on labs, Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges, and real-world attack simulations, HTB offers a fun and challenging way to level up your game.
